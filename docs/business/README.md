# PennyForge — Business Strategy Series

Realistic business strategy for PennyForge. Written to a hard honesty rule: **every revenue,
conversion, or market-size number in this series is a labeled estimate, assumption, target, or
scenario — not a prediction.** Competitor traction claims (e.g., Deal Soldier's reported revenue)
are treated as unverified unless sourced. Scenario math is shown so readers can rerun it with
their own assumptions.

## Core thesis

Penny items are the viral hook. Hidden clearance, receipt proof, route ROI, and reseller tools
are the paid retention engine. PennyForge does not try to out-scrape gray-data competitors; it
wins on trust, workflow, and community economics — see `docs/compliance.md` for the hard
boundaries every strategy in this series respects.
